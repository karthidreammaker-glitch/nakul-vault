NAKUL (നകുൽ)
"The handsome and wise" The fourth Pandava prince. A name symbolizing profound wisdom, extraordinary grace, and a deep connection with nature.
VEDANTH (വേദാന്ത്)
"The ultimate truth" The culmination of Vedic knowledge. It represents supreme spiritual wisdom, enlightenment, and the absolute cosmic reality.
